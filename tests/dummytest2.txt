Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming industries around the world. 
From healthcare and finance to education and transportation, AI systems are helping 
organizations automate repetitive tasks, analyze large amounts of data, and make 
better decisions.

Machine learning, a subset of AI, allows computers to learn patterns from data 
without being explicitly programmed. Companies are using machine learning models 
for recommendation systems, fraud detection, and predictive analytics.

However, the growth of AI also brings challenges. Ethical concerns such as bias 
in algorithms, privacy risks, and job displacement have become major topics of 
discussion among researchers and policymakers.

To address these issues, organizations are investing in responsible AI practices. 
These include building transparent models, ensuring fairness in datasets, and 
creating regulations that guide the safe development and deployment of AI systems.
